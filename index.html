Hello! I'm Sen, also known as senkodev.

Since the age of 9, I have been heavily invested in web and game development. Thanks to those early choices, I've grown up to become a full-stack developer and 3D artist.

Nowadays I am working for Senko Digital as a Chief Executive Officer and Lead Designer, primarily developing with Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, Node.js and Python for all of my projects.

I am also doing some side-hustles as an ambient musician and a part-time VRChat Avatar Creator.

Thanks to some random stranger I met in real life, I tried out Google Cardboard all the way back in 2015, which made me fall in love with VR, and I've been heavily invested in VR ever since. I have been active on VRChat since March 2018.

Currently rocking a Valve Index, 3 x 2.0 Base Stations and 4 x Vive 3.0 trackers for my VR setup.
